What to do in the quest
DANGEROUS QUEST This place is an underground 7-room's location where you have to find a way out and try not to get caught by a

When 2 people participate in the quest, then the keys are doubled




What to do to get out of the quest location
First you have to collect 4 keys which are hidden in all 7 rooms
But this is not easy to do for two reasons.
The first reason is the darkness, the location is so dark that you have to be careful when walking and running away from the maniac.
The second reason is, of course, the actor, who is dressed in a maniac costume and follows you with a chainsaw in his hands, and who knows the location like the back of his hand and hidden exits.
